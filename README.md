### Should you use AI? 

This is a short Twine Story designed to help my friends and family get a sense for how I'm thinking about AI use. 

My approach to the question in terms of an ethical question (Should I use it?) is the matter of responsibility, of a kind of informed consent about AI. 

What users will find is that I'm not making an ethical judgment from the same epistemology of "was this made under capitalism?" which always makes every possible action a bad one, and reduces ethical choices to critical lines of care for environment, theft and plagiarism, etc.

Instead, I draw the goalposts back to "Do you know what you're doing?"
